Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TitleCraft

❓ What is TitleCraft?

TitleCrafts is a free online text case converter tool that helps you format text instantly. Whether you need title case for your headlines, sentence case for paragraphs, or URL-friendly slugs, TitleCrafts makes it easy to format text with just one click.

🎯 Who can benefit from this tool?

TitleCraft is perfect for:

  • Content writers and bloggers
  • SEO professionals creating URL slugs
  • Social media managers
  • Students and academics
  • Developers needing text formatting
  • Anyone who works with text regularly

🔒 Is my text data secure?

Absolutely! All Text Conversion Happens Directly in Your Browser. Your Text Never Leaves Your Device or Is Sent to Any Server. We Do Not Store, Collect, or Access Any of Your Data. Your Privacy Is Completely Protected.

⚡ Why use TitleCraft over manual formatting?

Manual text formatting is time-consuming and error-prone. TitleCrafts saves you time by instantly converting text with professional accuracy. It follows industry-standard style guides (AP, Chicago), handles special cases like hyphens and apostrophes, and ensures consistency across all your content.

🌐 How does this help with SEO?

The hyphenation function instantly creates clean, SEO-friendly URL slugs. Proper title wrapping improves readability for titles and meta descriptions. Well-formatted content is easier for both users and search engines to understand, potentially improving your site's rankings.

💡 What makes TitleCraft different?

Unlike basic converters, TitleCrafts implements a smart title case algorithm that follows the AP and Chicago style guides. It handles edge cases like articles, prepositions, hyphenated words, and text after colons. Plus, it's fast, free, works offline, and respects your privacy with no data collection.
